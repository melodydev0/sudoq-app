SudoQ Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective: April 25, 2026  ·  Developer: Melody Yazılım  ·  App: SudoQ

This Privacy Policy explains how Melody Yazılım ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use the SudoQ mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Anonymous accounts: A randomly generated ID only — no personal data.
  • Google Sign-In: Display name, email address, and profile photo.
  • Sign in with Apple: Display name and email address (you may use Apple's private relay email).

Game Data

  • Game statistics, scores, win/loss records, levels, and achievements.
  • In-app purchase history (subscription status, not payment details).

Usage & Analytics

  • App events and session data via Firebase Analytics (screen views, feature usage, crash reports).
  • Crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics (device model, OS version, stack traces — no personal data).

Advertising (non-subscribers only)

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) — only if you grant tracking permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt on iOS.
  • If permission is denied, only contextual (non-personalized) ads are shown.

Push Notifications

  • Device push token (only if you grant notification permission).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the SudoQ game experience.
  • Authenticate your account and sync your progress across devices.
  • Verify in-app subscriptions and manage premium access.
  • Send game-related push notifications (daily challenges, match results) — only with your permission.
  • Show personalized or contextual ads to non-subscribers.
  • Diagnose and fix bugs and crashes.
  • Maintain leaderboards and multiplayer matchmaking.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for purposes unrelated to SudoQ.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account within the app:

  • All personal data, game history, and statistics are permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access – request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction – request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion – delete your account and all associated data via the in-app account deletion feature.
  • Portability – receive your data in a portable format.
  • Objection – opt out of personalized advertising (deny ATT permission on iOS or opt out in device settings).

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@sudoq.app.

6. Children's Privacy

SudoQ is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly. Please contact us if you believe a child has provided us with personal information.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption for data in transit and Firebase security rules for data at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. By using SudoQ, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for transfers from the EU/EEA in compliance with GDPR.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notice. The "Effective" date at the top reflects the latest revision.
